Ingredients

Dough

  • 100 gr butter (room temperature)
  • 75 g sugar
  • 150 gr low-fat quark
  • 300 gr flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 0.5 packet baking powder
  • 1 packet orange zest
  • 50 g chopped almonds
  • 150 g raisins

Coating

  • 125 gr powdered sugar
  • 100 gr butter
